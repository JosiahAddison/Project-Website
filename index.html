My Menu

  • Almond Croissants

    • A delicate pastry dusted with powdered sugar and baked to golden perfection.

    Ingredients

  • 1 sheet of puff pastry
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 4 cups bread flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt

    Recipe

  1. preheat oven 375*F
  2. roll out pastry pff and cut into triangles
  3. spread thin layer of almond paste on each
  4. roll each triangle from base to tip and curve into cresent
  5. brush with egg wash and sprinkle with powdered sugar
  6. bake 15-20min until it's golden brown

  • Sour Dough Bread

    • A community favorite!

    Ingredients

  • 1 cup sourdough starter
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • 4 cups bread flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt

    Recipe

  1. mix the starter,water, and 3 cups of flour to make sticky dough
  2. incorporate a tablespoon of flour
  3. Knead the dough
  4. place it in a greased bowl
  5. cover it
  6. let it rise for 5 hours
  7. shape it into a dough and leave it for 30 minutes
  8. bake till golden brown

  • Classic Carrot Cake

    • An homage to tradition.

    Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 cups grated carrots
  • 1 cup crushed pineapple
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts

    Recipe

  1. Preheat oven to 350*F
  2. Mix together flour, sugar, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt in large bowl
  3. Stir in oil and eggs till smooth
  4. Fold in carrots, pineapple, and walnuts
  5. Pour batter into greased baking pan
  6. Bake for 30-35min
  7. allow cake to cool before frosting with cream cheese
